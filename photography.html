  • When one is immersed in the midst of the biggest sadness and the problem, is not able to extrapolate its emotions, get out of there and see everything
    from a different perspective, one just believes that the world ends and life is over... it’s not like that; Today, I can finally say that I’ve learned the
    lesson, I’ve learned that life is about stages, things happen, life continues and nothing is eternal, I’ve learned that the periods for which someone goes
    through has to make the most of them because, life is full of opportunities; they are not always the same, not everything is equal and someone must get used
    to decide the best according to the parameters of each one. I think the hardest thing of each stage, is the moment in which ends up, my stage as Cup ended,
    I had to try to understand that I have gone to another stage and left behind everything from the previous one. Everything is based on decisions... I know
    it is not easy to discover when it ends one and begins another, I guess that one just knows it, but the best of everything is that now I can finally understand it,
    I mean things are over but one does not end with them, it simply CHANGES. Anyone who stuck in the past can not advance; all this time, my stay in the past was going
    to be too long, was not moving forward and was only a moment of illumination that made me wake up, life is full of experiences, one must take them, get the best out
    of them and build some better, it can not live of memories.
    So, this is my life. And I want you to know that I’m both happy and sad and I’m still trying to figure out how that could be.
